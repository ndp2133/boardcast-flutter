## Description

Stop guessing. Start surfing.

Boardcast scores your local surf conditions in real time — waves, wind, and tide — so you always know when to paddle out. No more checking three apps and still guessing wrong.

YOUR CONDITIONS, SCORED
Get a single match score (0-100) based on your preferences. See at a glance whether it's Epic, Good, Fair, or Poor for your skill level and favorite conditions.

SEE THE FULL WEEK
Interactive charts show wave height, wind speed, and tide curves. Daily forecast cards with swell direction, water temp, wind quality, and energy levels. Your best window is highlighted automatically.

PLAN YOUR SESSION
Hour-by-hour conditions grid for the next 7 days. Pick the perfect time to surf based on real data, not vibes.

AI SURF COACH
Get personalized tips from an AI coach that knows your conditions. Ask natural language questions like "When should I surf tomorrow?" and get instant answers.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Log sessions, rate conditions, and build your Surf IQ over time. The more you surf, the smarter Boardcast gets at matching your preferences.

LOCATIONS
Currently covering 11 spots across New York, New Jersey, California, and Florida — with more locations added regularly. Built by surfers who have to work for it.

Free to use with basic features. Upgrade to Premium for AI coaching, unlimited queries, push alerts, and the home screen widget.
